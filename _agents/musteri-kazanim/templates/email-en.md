## 💡 Personalization Rules

1. **Sound natural** — Avoid overly formal corporate language, keep it conversational
2. **Opening hook max 2 sentences** — Get to the point quickly
3. **Be specific** — Don't say "I love your content", say "Your latest AI tool comparison got 500K views"
4. **Social proof** — Lead with metrics and results (views, brands, case studies)
5. **One CTA per email** — Don't overwhelm with multiple asks
6. **Emoji** — Subject line emoji is fine, keep body professional
7. **A/B test** — Create 2-3 subject line variants per campaign
